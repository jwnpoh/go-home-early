Go Home Early is a utility to help subject coordinators go home early. Go Home Early provides a few functions to help coordinators perform various routine batch actions related to results administration quickly.

Generate

Generates separate marksheets according tutor or class from a blank central results template.

Record

Records marks from separate marksheets submitted by tutors onto central results template.

Cockpit

Copies the total mark from your central results list onto Cockpit-generated CSV templates, which can then be uploaded back to Cockpit directly.

Analyse

Analyses results data.
